Frequently Asked Questions

What types of videos can I transcribe?

You can transcribe any YouTube video that has closed captions or subtitles enabled. This includes most professionally produced content and videos with auto-generated captions.

How accurate are the transcripts?

The accuracy depends on the video's audio quality and the availability of manual captions. For videos with clear audio and manual captions, accuracy is typically above 95%.

Is there a limit to video length?

Yes, we currently support videos up to 1 hour in length. For longer videos, you may need to process them in segments.

How long are results stored?

Results are temporarily stored and automatically deleted after download or after 1 hour. We recommend downloading your results immediately.
